About Me

I grew up in West TN where I attended The University of Tennessee at Martin. I graduated in 2016 with my undergraduate degree in Business Administration with a focus in Management. My degree led me to sales primarily in the supply-chain sector. I found success in sales and thoroughly enjoyed working with people and companies of all different backgrounds, but I knew that I wanted to sharpen my technical skillset and impact companies in new and exciting ways.

I knew of several colleagues from my undergraduate experience that had transitioned to software development through way of Nashville Software School with great success. When I felt the sting of unemployment due to the COVID pandemic, I decided to use this time to bet on myself and take the leap into software. The experience has been nothing short of amazing. I feel prepared to come out of this experience with a broader toolbox of skills and ready to make a meaningul impact.

Wanderlust

Wanderlust was created for those that are in need of a fully-functional and interactive web application to store travel destinations. Wanderlust creates multiple views for user input including a wish list, a planned list, and a completed list to provide a clean and concise travel journal experience. Single-page app has full CRUD functionality and was created utilizing React, Bootstrap, and API fetch calls to JSON server. Google Maps API and developer tools were deployed to make the app more dynamic and to provide the user with a visual representation via an interactive map. See more here
